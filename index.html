Wonderbook is an app for iPhone and Android, built for book lovers, by book lovers.

library_books

Scan books to trade

If you have books around the house you no longer need, pare down. Use the barcode scanner to give away books to earn wonderbook credits you can use to receive books.

smartphone

Swipe right on books

On the wonderbook mobile app, swipe through books selected for you. Swipe right to add to your wishlist, and you'll get it in the mail when it becomes available.

markunread_mailbox

Quick & Easy

When it's time for you to mail your books, we'll do the heavy lifting. You'll get a prepostaged envelope so you can drop your book in the mail to its new owner.

About

Yonderbook is currently under development and is expected to launch in 2018. If you are a developer and would like to contribute, visit the github repo.
